# Decision Log

Use this log for significant roadmap changes. Record the reason and trade-off
when you defer a certification, change market-lane priorities, add a new goal,
or remove work from the plan.

This is not a task list. Small weekly adjustments belong in
[now.md](now.md).

## Decision Template

Copy this section when making a decision.

### YYYY-MM-DD - Short Decision Title

| Field | Notes |
|---|---|
| Decision | What changed? |
| Reason | What evidence or constraint caused the change? |
| Trade-off | What becomes less important, deferred or cancelled? |
| Review date | When should this decision be reconsidered? |
| Related evidence | Link to market log, quarterly review or relevant artefact. |
