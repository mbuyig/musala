## Review Template

Copy this section when a quarterly review is due.

### YYYY-MM-DD

#### Progress

- What did I complete?
- What slipped?
- What should be cancelled, not just delayed?

#### Market Evidence

- What did recruiters respond to?
- What keywords appear in job descriptions?
- What rates am I seeing?
- Is Lane A producing enough opportunities?
- Are Lane B or C producing stronger opportunities?
- Is FinOps demand stronger than expected?
- Is CKS becoming commercially important?

#### Delivery Health

- Is SAP on track?
- Are lab costs under control?
- Does my CV look like a consultant profile?
- Do I have strong case studies?
- Am I developing consulting and sales behaviours?

#### Decisions

- What changes should I make to the next quarter's focus?
- What will I explicitly stop doing?
- What are the next quarter's top three outcomes?
